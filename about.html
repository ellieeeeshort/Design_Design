We are a collective of students at Goldsmiths University who get together once a week for design related inspiration, workshops, talks and networking.

In a social, informal setting, we hope to broaden our Design horizons, keeping fresh with Design excitement and pushing our links with industry further than the University.

Come join us on Mondays 6pm - 7:30pm, we post on our Facebook page the room each week and sometimes we end up at the pub.
